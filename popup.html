MessengerJump

Jump to any date in Messenger

1
Go to facebook.com and open a Messenger conversation.
2
Click the blue calendar button in the bottom-right corner of the page.
3
Pick a date or date range, then hit Search messages.
4
If no results appear, scroll up in the conversation to load older messages, then search again.
Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+D
Works on facebook.com and web.facebook.com
