MessengerJump

Jump to any date in Messenger

Extension enabled ON — active on Facebook
1
Go to facebook.com and open a Messenger conversation.
2
Click the blue calendar button in the bottom-right corner.
3
Pick a date or date range and hit Search messages.
4
The extension auto-scrolls to load older messages until your date is found.
Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+Shift+D
Works on facebook.com and web.facebook.com
